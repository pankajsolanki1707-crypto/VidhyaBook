Serving Indore's Aspirants Since 2005

Vidhya Book Store was founded with a clear objective: to support civil service aspirants and DAVV university students in Bhanwarkuan. Situated in Payal Plaza right below the famous Kautilya Academy, our store has become a key study hub for students preparing for crucial state and national examinations.

We work directly with authors and top academies to ensure our students get official publications, authentic test series prints, and up-to-date competitive material without any compromises. We also support eco-friendly academic cycles through our used book buyback and exchange platform, helping lower educational costs for all.
